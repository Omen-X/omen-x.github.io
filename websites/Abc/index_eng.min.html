OUR COMPANY

2008

The birthday of the Company is considered to be considered April 30, 2008. At that time, the company's activities included the production of security cameras and the implementation of video surveillance equipment.

2009 – 2011

The staff of the company is expanding. The main efforts are aimed at determining the product line being sold, and further - at conquering regional sales markets. Formed an active client group using analog video surveillance systems.

In 2011, the first retail outlet store opened, named after the Company's main brand in Moscow

2012

An important event for the company during this period was the introduction into production of high-quality components of its own design, manufactured according to our specifications at factories in Korea and Taiwan, and as a result - an increase in the warranty period for equipment up to two years. The range of analog equipment is expanding, while a new direction is being formed: the production of IP equipment. Production capacity increases - a new production line is launched in Voronezh with a capacity of up to 50,000 units. products per quarter.

A new office is opening in China, brand stores in Voronezh and Novosibirsk.

2013

Active development continues. New projects are launched and new directions are opened. This year was the Company introduces into the product line a new brand - a manufacturer of video surveillance for the general consumer.

In December 2013, we launched our own training center aimed at organizing and conducting internal and external training (company employees, dealers and partners staff) in the basics of building video surveillance systems and security technologies.

2014

One of the important moments in the life of the company was the opening of the new Central Office. A new direction in the activities of the third brand, specializing in the production of unified professional equipment for government orders and tender purchases, was launched. The company takes part in the federal project "Safe City". Actively developing direction for working with the HOA and the Criminal Code.

To support the level of quality, service and innovative approach to the production and sale of products, our employees are constantly trained in specialized seminars and courses.

2016

For 8 years, the company has emerged and confidently occupies a leading position in the Russian video surveillance market, continuing to actively develop and successfully develop new areas. The number of employees exceeded 150 people and continues to grow steadily, constantly replenishing new valuable personnel. During all this time, the company (despite the crisis, general bankruptcies and reductions) has significantly increased its production, sphere of presence and influence in the market.

The company began, like most similar business structures, with the resale of equipment, now we are in the top 10 largest manufacturers of video surveillance equipment in Russia, producing and selling an average of about 15,000 pieces of equipment per month.

2017

Despite the complicated external factors of business development, the Company clearly understands and strictly moves towards its strategic goals. In addition to the development of three existing brands, the Company is actively developing a new brand with a focus on the end user, significantly expanding the soy geography of its presence in the retail and retail segments. Our partners include such partners as the ABC-Electro and Leroy Merlin network.
